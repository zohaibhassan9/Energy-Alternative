Residential Systems

Power your home, cut bills, and reduce your carbon footprint. Our rooftop solar systems include battery storage and net metering options, letting you store extra energy and even sell it back to the grid.

Commercial Systems

Smart energy for smart businesses. We provide scalable solar solutions for offices, factories, and institutions—cutting operational costs and promoting sustainability. Optional PPA available: No upfront cost, just clean energy at a fixed rate.

Agricultural Systems

Solar pumps that bring water to life. Ideal for rural and off-grid areas, our solar pumping systems power irrigation and livestock needs—fuel-free, eco-friendly, and cost-effective.

EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction)

From blueprint to power-on—leave it to us. We manage your entire solar project: designing, sourcing, installing, and maintaining high-performance systems on time and within budget.

PPA (Power Purchase Agreement)

Go solar with zero investment. With PPA, we install and maintain the system while you only pay for the energy at a fixed rate—lower than your utility bills.

Bank Leasing Projects

Solar energy without upfront costs. Our partnerships with banks let you lease a solar system, saving money while we handle ownership, maintenance, and installation.
