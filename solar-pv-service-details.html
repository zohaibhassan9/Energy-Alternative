Residential Solar Systems

Transform your home into an energy-efficient powerhouse. Our residential solar solutions are designed to maximize your energy independence while reducing your carbon footprint and electricity bills.

Our expert team designs and installs premium rooftop solar systems that seamlessly integrate with your home's architecture. We offer complete solutions including high-efficiency solar panels, battery storage systems, and smart energy management with net metering capabilities.

With our residential systems, you can store excess energy during peak production times and either use it when needed or sell it back to the grid, creating an additional revenue stream while ensuring uninterrupted power supply.

Commercial Solar Solutions

Power your business with clean, cost-effective energy. Our commercial solar solutions are engineered to deliver maximum efficiency and rapid return on investment for businesses of all sizes.

We specialize in scalable solar solutions for offices, manufacturing facilities, retail spaces, and institutions. Our systems are designed to significantly reduce your operational costs while demonstrating your commitment to sustainability.

For businesses looking to avoid upfront capital expenditure, we offer Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) options where you pay only for the energy produced at rates typically lower than utility prices, with no installation or maintenance costs.

Agricultural Solar Applications

Revolutionize your farming operations with solar power. Our agricultural solutions provide reliable, off-grid power for irrigation and livestock needs without fuel costs or emissions.

Ideal for rural and remote farming operations, our solar pumping systems deliver consistent water supply for irrigation and livestock, eliminating dependence on unreliable grid power or expensive diesel generators.

EPC Services (Engineering, Procurement & Construction)

Our turnkey EPC service handles every aspect of your solar project from initial feasibility studies to final commissioning. We manage system design, equipment procurement, installation, and ongoing maintenance, ensuring optimal performance and compliance with all regulations.

Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) Model

The PPA model allows you to benefit from solar energy with no upfront investment. We install, own, and maintain the system on your property, while you purchase the generated electricity at predictable rates typically 10-30% below utility prices.

Bank Leasing Options

Through our partnerships with leading financial institutions, we offer attractive leasing options that make solar power accessible without large capital outlay. Our leasing programs include full installation, maintenance, and performance guarantees.
