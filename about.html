About Energy Alternatives

Energy Alternatives is a highly diversified and progressive company involved in the business of sales & Services for Renewable energy projects in all sectors of Pakistan, including Residential, Agricultural & industrial solar power plants and equipment.
Energy Alternatives also engaged in engineering design Plant construction and fabrication activities.
Energy Alternatives is managed by a team of professionals including sale personnel and project engineers responsible to deliver to the world’s leading brands of technologies and equipment’s having sufficient experience of project management. Company provide total turnkey solutions to our valuable customers starting from concept, engineering design, project execution and management, after sales support and services.

⠀Vision

At Energy Alternatives, we envision a future where clean, renewable energy powers every corner of Pakistan, driving economic growth, environmental preservation and energy independence. Our vision is to lead this transformation by providing innovative, high-performance energy solutions that empower industries, communities and individuals to embrace a sustainable lifestyle. We aspire to become the benchmark for renewable energy excellence in Pakistan, setting the standard for quality, innovation and reliability.

⠀Mission

Energy Alternatives’ mission is to revolutionize Pakistan’s energy future by delivering sustainable, high performance renewable energy solutions that empower communities and drive economic progress. We are committed to accelerating the adoption of clean energy, reducing the nation’s carbon footprint and helping build a greener, more resilient Pakistan. Quality is at the core of everything we do. From our exclusive on-site testing facility to our in-house engineering and fabrication capabilities, we ensure every project is executed with precision and meets the highest standards of durability, safety and performance. We strive to make clean energy accessible and affordable for all, helping individuals and organizations reduce their environmental impact and move toward energy independence.

Trust in us

Energy Alternatives has 12 years, splendid history of exclusive distribution of world’s leading prestigious technology partners all over the world particularly from Germany, US, Turkey, China etc. in Pakistan. Energy Alternatives enjoys status of diversified business preposition having departments in engineering, fabrication, trading, servicing and consultation sectors of Pakistan.

Energy Alternatives has intensive sales and services network of qualified and experienced professional teams throughout Pakistan. Energy Alternatives evidences a continuing growth and success with large base of satisfied loyal customers in industries of solar power plants, solar thermal plants, fabrication, and power generation sector. Energy Alternatives believes in achieving business targets in win-win methodology, sharing our success with customers and principals.

Win - win

We are committed to accomplish our mission of transforming our business into a win – win game for our principals, customers and ourselves.

Business Approach

Our people excel in any environment to pursue customer satisfaction through exquisite delivery that guarantees optimum profits for clients and robust growth for ourselves.
