Frequently Ask Question

We have been operating for over a decade, providing top-notch services to our clients and building a strong track record.

How long would it take to recover my investment?

SRE's customized solar solutions offer significant savings and typically pay back in under 4 years due to rising electricity rates and other factors.

How does the system efficiency change over time?

Total efficiency drop in 15 years will be 11%. After 25 years, the panels will still be working at 80% efficiency.

What happens when the weather is cloudy?

Even on overcast days, the system provides significant heating. In fog, clouds, or rain, the system switches to an alternate source if necessary.

How often is the maintenance required?

This system is almost maintenance free. Occasional washing of solar panels is required to attain the maximum efficiency possible.
